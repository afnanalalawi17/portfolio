About Me

Hello! My name is Afnan

“If you are not going to tell the world who you are, the world is not going to tell you, how good you are.”

A mobile app developer with over 4 years of experience, specializing in building applications using Flutter, with solid experience in working with MySQL databases.
I have contributed to the development and implementation of several projects across diverse domains such as government services, human resources systems, appointment booking platforms, food delivery applications, and safety monitoring tools.

I also earned first place in the Hajj and Umrah Challenge organized by the Ministry of Hajj and Umrah in recognition of my digital innovation contributions.
I have strong teamwork, problem-solving, and self-learning skills. I’ve participated in tech programs and competitions such as Google Developer Student Club, and attended cybersecurity and programming bootcamps and hackathons.
I aspire to continually enhance my skills and contribute meaningfully in environments that encourage innovation and digital transformation.
